Overview

Purpose

The purpose of our company is to provide fun, safe, and educational white water rafting trips. We pride ourselves in our amazing staff. We strive to nurture and grow feelings of love and respect for the water in both youth and adults. In order to market the strong values of our company we have decided to create this website. The website will draw attention to our company. Ultimately, we hope to see increased revenue and happy customers.

Audience

Our target demographic is families with children as well as church groups and school groups. These demographics have a primary concern for safety. We assure our customers that our staff are experienced and competent white water rafting tour guides. This helps to ease the minds of many anxious parents and teachers accompanying the children who need to know that their children will be safe. Our website will be accessible via mobile phone, desktop and, laptop.
